## Top Stories

1. Anthropic said it will sue the Department of Defense after the Pentagon formally designated the AI company a supply-chain risk—the first such label ever applied to an American firm. The dispute stems from a collapsed contract: Anthropic refused to grant the military control over its models for use in autonomous weapons and mass domestic surveillance, after which the Pentagon turned to OpenAI. CEO Dario Amodei said most commercial customers are unaffected, but Microsoft confirmed it will keep Anthropic's tools in its client products after concluding the designation applies only to defense work.
