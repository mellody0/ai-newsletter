4. Nvidia plans to invest $2 billion each in photonics companies Lumentum and Coherent, committing $4 billion in total to support their research and US manufacturing operations. Photonic interconnects—components that move data using light rather than electrical signals—are central to reducing latency and power consumption in large AI data centers, making the investment a strategic one for Nvidia's infrastructure ambitions. ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/technology/nvidia-invest-2-billion-photonic-product-maker-lumentum-2026-03-02/))
